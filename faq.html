Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Exercise Tips

How to avoid execution errors in Squats?

To avoid execution errors in Squats:

  • Ensure proper form by keeping your back straight, chest up, and knees in line with your toes.
  • Start with lighter weights to master the technique before increasing resistance.
  • Avoid rounding your back or letting your knees cave inwards during the movement.
How to perform Abdominals correctly?

To perform Abdominals correctly:

  • Engage your core muscles throughout the exercise.
  • Avoid pulling on your neck and focus on using your abdominal muscles to lift your upper body.
  • Exhale as you lift and inhale as you lower your torso.
How to avoid mistakes in Jumping Jacks?

To avoid mistakes in Jumping Jacks:

  • Land softly with slightly bent knees to reduce impact on joints.
  • Keep your arms and legs straight throughout the movement.
  • Focus on maintaining a steady pace and rhythm.
How to prevent errors in Jump Rope?

To prevent errors in Jump Rope:

  • Use a rope of appropriate length and adjust it if necessary.
  • Land softly on the balls of your feet to reduce strain on your ankles.
  • Start with slow, controlled jumps and gradually increase speed as you improve.
How to avoid common mistakes in Running?

To avoid common mistakes in Running:

  • Maintain proper posture with your head up, shoulders relaxed, and arms bent at a 90-degree angle.
  • Focus on breathing rhythmically and evenly.
  • Avoid overstriding and aim for a cadence of around 180 steps per minute.
